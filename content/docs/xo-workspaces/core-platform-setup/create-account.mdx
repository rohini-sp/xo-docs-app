## What You'll Need

- A valid email address
- A GitHub, Google, or GitLab account for authentication

## Sign Up

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the Sign-Up Page

    Go to [beta.xo.builders/sign-up](https://beta.xo.builders/sign-up). You'll see the registration screen with sign-in options.

    ![XO sign-up page](/images/core-platform-setup/create-account-1.jpeg)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Enter Your Username

    Click the **Username** field and type your desired username.

    ![Click the Username field](/images/core-platform-setup/create-account-2.jpeg)

    <Callout type="warn">
      Usernames can only contain **letters**, **numbers**, **hyphens (-)**, and **underscores (_)**. No spaces or special characters.
    </Callout>

    ![Username format example](/images/core-platform-setup/create-account-3.jpeg)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create Your Account

    Click **Create your account** to finalize registration.

    ![Click Create your account](/images/core-platform-setup/create-account-4.jpeg)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Pick Your Plan

    Select the plan that fits your needs. The **Basic** plan is free and gets you started immediately.

    ![Pick your plan](/images/core-platform-setup/create-account-5.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Next?

Your account is ready. Head to the [dashboard](https://beta.xo.builders) to create your first workspace, or follow the [Access and Restart Your Machine](/docs/xo-workspaces/core-platform-setup/access-restart-machine) guide to configure your environment.
