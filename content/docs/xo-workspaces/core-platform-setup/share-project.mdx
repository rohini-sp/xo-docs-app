Invite team members to collaborate on your workspace by granting them access through their email address. You can also revoke access at any time.

## What You'll Need

- An [XO account](/docs/xo-workspaces/core-platform-setup/create-account) with an active workspace
- The exact email address of the person you want to invite

## Grant Access

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the Share Dialog

    From your project dashboard, click **Share Project**.

    ![Click Share Project](/images/core-platform-setup/share-project-1.jpeg)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Search by Email

    Type the collaborator's **exact email address** in the search field.

    ![Search by email address](/images/core-platform-setup/share-project-2.jpeg)

    <Callout type="warn">
      The email must match exactly. Partial matches or usernames won't work — use the full email address the collaborator registered with.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm Access

    Click **Grant Access** to give them access to your workspace.

    ![Click Grant Access](/images/core-platform-setup/share-project-3.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Revoke Access

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Find the Collaborator

    In the same share dialog, locate the collaborator you want to remove and click **Revoke**.

    ![Click Revoke](/images/core-platform-setup/share-project-4.jpeg)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm Revocation

    Click **Revoke Access** to confirm. The collaborator loses access immediately.

    ![Click Revoke Access](/images/core-platform-setup/share-project-5.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Revoking access is instant. The collaborator won't be able to open the workspace or its IDE after removal.
</Callout>
